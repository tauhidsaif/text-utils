About Us

Welcome to TextUtils! Our app allows you to manipulate text in multiple ways: convert to uppercase, lowercase, capitalize words, sentence case, reverse text, remove extra spaces, copy to clipboard, or download as a file. We aim to make text editing fast, intuitive, and visually engaging.

Uppercase & Lowercase

Convert text to uppercase or lowercase quickly and efficiently.

Capitalize Words

Automatically capitalize the first letter of each word for neat text formatting.

Sentence Case

Automatically format your sentences with proper capitalization.

Reverse Text

Reverse your text instantly for fun or specific formatting needs.

Remove Spaces

Clean up extra spaces in your text with a single click.

Copy & Download

Easily copy text to clipboard or download it as a file for later use.
